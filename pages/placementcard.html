Job Description & Responsibilities

We are looking for passionate Software Development Engineers to join our core infrastructure team. You will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining high-performance, scalable systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and deploy scalable software solutions using modern cloud architectures.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code in C++, C#, or Java.
  • Conduct code reviews and contribute to engineering best practices.

Requirements

  • Strong understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms.
  • Experience with object-oriented design and programming.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work independently.
